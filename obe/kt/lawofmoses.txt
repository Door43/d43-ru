====== Закон, Закон Моисеев, Божий Закон ======

===== Определение: =====

Понятия «Закон» и «Закон Моисеев», в основном, относятся ко всем заповедям и наставлениям, которые Бог дал Моисею и народу израильскому для соблюдения. По-другому эти законы можно назвать «Законом Господним» или «Божьим Законом».

  * В зависимости от контекста, слово «Закон» или «Закон Божий» может относиться к:
      * десяти заповедям, которые Бог написал для израильтян на каменных скрижалях,
      * ко всем постановлениям, данным Моисею,
      * к Пятикнижию Моисея,
      * ко всему Ветхому Завету,
      * ко всем Божьм заповедям и Его воле.
  * Иногда выражение "Закон и Пророки" используется, подразумевая Ветхий Завет.

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * Since there are many instructions, some translations may prefer to translate this in the plural and say, "laws."
  * The "Law of Moses" could be translated as "the laws that God told Moses to give to the Israelites".
  * "God's law(s)" can be translated as "the laws that God gave" or "the laws that God commanded."
  * Depending on the context, "the Law of Moses" can also be translated as "the law that Moses wrote" or "the books that Moses wrote" or "the laws that God told Moses to give to the Israelites."
  * The term "the Law" could also be translated as "God's commands" or "everything that God commanded" or "God's rules."

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:ru:obs:notes:frames:13-07|[13-07]]]** God also gave many other **laws**  and rules to follow. If the people obeyed these **laws**, God promised that he would bless and protect them. If they disobeyed them, God would punish them.
  * **[[:ru:obs:notes:frames:13-09|[13-09]]]** Anyone who disobeyed **God's law**  could bring an animal to the altar in front of the Tent of Meeting as a sacrifice to God.
  * **[[:ru:obs:notes:frames:15-13|[15-13]]]** Then Joshua reminded the people of their obligation to obey the covenant that God had made with the Israelites at Sinai. The people promised to remain faithful to God and follow **his laws**.
  * **[[:ru:obs:notes:frames:16-01|[16-01]]]** After Joshua died, the Israelites disobeyed God and did not drive out the rest of the Canaanites or obey **God's laws**.
  * **[[:ru:obs:notes:frames:21-05|[21-05]]]** In the New Covenant, God would write **his law**  on the people's hearts, the people would know God personally, they would be his people, and God would forgive their sins.
  * **[[:ru:obs:notes:frames:27-01|[27-01]]]** Jesus answered, "What is written in **God's law**?"
  * **[[:ru:obs:notes:frames:28-01|[28-01]]]** Jesus said to him, "Why do you call me 'good?' There is only one who is good, and that is God. But if you want to have eternal life, obey **God's laws**."
